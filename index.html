About ClouDiary

Private and secure

Only you should be able to read your diary. That should be the case if it's in the cloud, or tucked under your pillow.

For ClouDiary, your privacy is paramount. State of the art technology is used to ensure that your private data is kept private. Only you can see it and that will never change.

Free!

Yes, that's right! FREE!

There's no cost to store you diary in the cloud with ClouDiary.

In the future, there will be cool features and extras that there may be a small charge for. For now, it will not cost you penny!

Built with love

No one likes using buggy apps and no one likes writing buggy code!

The whole app has been built from the ground up, using open source libraries and software. A significant amount of time has been taken to build the app "the right way" from the start so you should expect the app to be as fast, reliable and as secure as it possibly can be.
